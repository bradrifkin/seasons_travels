What is some of the best advice for inexperienced travelers?

Great question! Here are some travel tips:


  • Make sure you keep your travel documents (e.g. passport, ID, tickets, etc.) close to you at all times. When you leave the hotel, make sure they are locked up.
  • Consider activities away from the crowds. For example, if in Paris, see the Eiffel Tower (of course!), but a walking tour with a local resident will teach you so much about the city.
  • Before your trip, create a to-do list of everything that needs to be done, such as packing for outdoors activities, convert money to local currency, etc.

For more tips, please feel free to contact us!
